French toast

Great way to start your day

This easy recipe works with many types of bread - white, whole wheat, brioche, cinnamon-raisin, Italian, or French. Serve hot with butter or margarine and maple syrup.

What's needed

  • 6 thick slices bread
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2/3 cup milk
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract (Optional)
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground cinnamon (Optional)
  • salt to taste
  • 1 tablespoon unsalted butter, or more as needed

Simple to make

  1. Whisk milk, eggs, vanilla, cinnamon, and salt together in a bowl.
  2. Lightly butter a griddle and heat over medium-high heat.
  3. Dunk bread in the mixture. Transfer to skillet and cook until golden. Serve hot.
