Omelette

Healthy and delicious

A Healthy Omelette recipe that tastes as fresh and delicious as it looks! A folded golden omelette, stuffed with sauteed mushrooms, spinach and melty mozzarella. An easy and healthy breakfast!

You will need

  • 3 eggs
  • 1 cup kale
  • 1 cup portobello mushrooms
  • 1 tbsp fresh onions
  • 1 tbsp butter
  • 1 tbsp extra virgin olive oil
  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese

Step by step

  1. In a non stick pan add the olive oil, chopped mushrooms and fresh onion.
  2. Saute for 7-8 minutes on medium heat.
  3. Remove from pan and set aside.
  4. In the same pan add the kale leaves. Saute for 10 minutes on medium-low heat. Remove from pan and set aside.
  5. In a small bowl crack the two eggs and whisk well.
  6. Melt butter in pan on medium heat.
  7. Add the beaten eggs and let the omelette cook for 7 minutes on medium heat.
  8. Carefully flip the omelette and let it cook for 5 minutes.
  9. Add the mozzarella on one half of the omelette and let it melt for 2 minutes.
  10. Add the kale and mushrooms over the melted mozzarella.
  11. Flip half of the omelette over the cheese, kale and mushrooms.
  12. Serve while still warm!.
